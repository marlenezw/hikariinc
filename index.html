A Message From Our CoFounder

Hello!

We are so glad that you are considering going on this adventure with us, Hikari Inc. We love adventures and being a part of great stories such as yours. This reminds me of a story when someone became a part of my adventure. I remember the frustration in my final High School year, trying to study and not being able to recall anything. I wondered what’s the point of studying. Perhaps that’s just like you, that there are times when you’ve felt stuck or just not having clarity on the next step, or simply wanting to give up. As I walked home from the study that day, I bumped into the Deputy Headmaster who said, ‘I have seen you go to the study everyday, it will pay off.’ I expressed my frustrations, my pain and my plan to throw in the towel. He encouraged me, ‘Do not stop, your efforts will not go to waste!’. I committed & purposed it not to give up, the rest is history. Years later, I reflect on the lesson and wisdom learnt, not to give up and that every bit of effort counts even when it doesn’t make sense. I now really believe that the key to succeeding in life is not because we’re smarter, faster or stronger But it’s in having people who have gone before, like parents, teachers, a colleague, whose experience has been shaped differently to ours, to help guide and point us to opportunities that could have otherwise been missed. This helping hand from others, combined with discovering your God given Purpose and Calling, having a clear Vision that you commit to with all your heart, mind, body and soul, once these ingredients are combined, there is no stopping you from achieving your goals and dreams. We, Hikari Inc are passionate about helping leaders, whether, just starting off, in the middle or seasoned in business, because there are times in life where we all feel, we’re not growing, we feel fearful, or we just don’t know how to take the next step. It’s in those moments it counts to have someone to help and remind us of our purpose, our reason for existence. Hikari Inc is there to help you in your journey, this adventure you are on, to move you form success to significance by sharing with you our combined experience and expertise that will help you go further faster.
With my very best,

Jacob and Team
