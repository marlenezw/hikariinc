What We Offer

Hikari Technology Design is the highly innovative arm of the business that is currently developing capabilities to create technologies and infrastructure to power client and partner businesses and systems aligned to the digital era. Any business without a digital strategy and digital suite will be left behind in the age of AI, Big Data, algorithms and the ever-growing online communities. Hikari Inc is positioning to take you and your business to tomorrow through Technology Design, ‘the practice of creating technology products, services and environments, a key process of innovation and development.’

How We Do This

Our technology consultancy provides ready to use solutions for your digital needs. This includes:

  • Ready to use software packages: To help your business take full advantage of the online market.
  • On demand tech talent: Our team of developers and engineers is ready to take your projects. We provide you with the talent to take you from idea to product.
  • Data and Analytics: Pivoting to a data driven business model is one of the best things your company can do. We provide you with the tools and support you need to be able to do this

    • Community Impact
