Our Business, Why We Exist

Hikari Inc exists to inspire, equip and develop leaders to go further faster, propelling them and their organizations to move from a place of success to significance while leading with integrity.

Our vision is to see leaders impact purposefully beyond their own business, organization or field, to communities and society at large, using dynamic agile business strategies & tools integrated with technology. Through engagement, consultation and training, leaders are challenged to go beyond their frame of thinking and motivated to take bold action that brings personal transformation, inevitably permeating into other areas of business & life.

In a world where Technology and Digital Communication have become an integral part of our lives, Hikari Inc believes that for companies and organizations of the digital age to thieve, they need to deconstruct the traditional business set-up, structures and leadership paradigms. These need to be reconstructed with agile methodologies and digital business frameworks that give reach and capabilities beyond the traditional models. Agility is key and dynamic strategies that respond timely and or ahead of market disruptions.

As technology continues to become an underpinning factor of success for businesses, the world demands that we get a handle of this as a continent. Hikari Inc has a keen interest to see Africa develop, ‘Rise’ and take up its place to impact and make a significant contribution on the world stage. This cannot be realized without a heart of generosity, sharing and a common universal vision to see our communities prosper through businesses of integrity. We exist to create the connection and enable such leadership and businesses.

What We Do

Hikari Inc is passionate about developing purpose driven leaders who desire to impact beyond themselves while running profitable growing businesses. For the most part applied human potential is limitless, and we are all about shedding light on human potential. Through our business framework, we take leaders to the next level of success and performance. Our three business pillars, Hikari Leadership, Hikari Accelerate and Hikari Technology Design, work together to bring out the best in the leader and their organization by providing modern and effective tools, principles and strategies for businesses, through Dynamic Leadership Training, Business Development and Consulting.
